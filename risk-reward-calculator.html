Formula notes

  • Per-trade mode: dollar risk = total account size × risk per trade (%).
  • Portfolio split mode: trade budget = (total account size × portfolio risk %) ÷ planned trades.
  • Risk per share = entry price - stop price + slippage per share.
  • Position size is rounded down from usable risk dollars ÷ risk per share.
  • Reward/risk uses slippage-adjusted reward and risk.
